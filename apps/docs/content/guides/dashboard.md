# Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you will be greeted by the **Tenant Admin Dashboard**. This dashboard serves as the central command center for your educational network, allowing you to manage multiple school memberships, monitor aggregated statistics, and delegate administrative duties.

## Multi-School Membership Management

If you manage multiple schools or branches under your account, your dashboard aggregates this data in one place:

- **Membership List**: View all active tenant memberships and organizations associated with your administrator account.
- **Aggregated Statistics**: The overview page displays real-time statistics (e.g., total registered students, active instructors, active class sections) across all your schools.
- **Switching Contexts**: Seamlessly jump between different school memberships to perform specific administrative tasks.

## Administrative Options & Invitations

As the owner/creator of the tenant organizations, you have two options for managing each membership:

1. **Direct Administration**: You can act as the active administrator for each school membership yourself, configuring academic terms, programs, and enrolling students directly.
2. **Delegate via Admin Invites**:
   - You can send an email invitation to another user to become an administrator for a specific school.
   - Once the invited administrator registers and accepts the invite, they will take over the onboarding and daily management of that school.

## Key Modules

The left navigation sidebar provides access to the core modules of the active school portal:

### 1. Overview

The main landing page showing:

- Real-time aggregated statistics of your school(s).
- Quick action buttons to register new users, build courses, or configure settings.
- Recent system logs and administrative updates.

### 2. Academics

Manage the academic structure of the active school:

- **Academic Terms**: Define semesters or academic years (e.g., _Fall 2026_, _Spring 2027_) with start and end dates.
- **Programs**: Set up departments or academic tracks (e.g., _High School Curriculum_, _Computer Science Program_).
- **Courses**: Add individual subjects belonging to a program (e.g., _Algebra I_, _Introduction to Physics_).
- **Classes**: Create specific class cohorts (e.g., _Class 10-A_, _Physics Lab Section 2_) and assign primary instructors.

### 3. User Management & Onboarding

Manage accounts and roles:

- **Administrators**: Invite and manage additional administrators to help run the school.
- **Instructors**: Register teachers, assign them to classes, and manage their schedules.
- **Students**: Onboard students, assign roll numbers, record admission dates, and link them to their guardians.
- **Parents/Guardians**: Store parent/guardian emergency contact details and associate them with their children.

### 4. Settings

Adjust school-specific settings, including:

- School display name, contact details, and logo.
- Custom grading scales.
- Notification configurations.
