# School Registration

Getting started with Gurukul is simple. As a school owner or administrator, you can set up a custom portal for your institution in just a few steps.

## Onboarding Steps

### 1. Visit the Registration Page

Go to the Gurukul homepage and click on **Get Started** or **Register School** (typically located at the main domain, e.g., `gurukul.com/signup`).

### 2. Configure Your Custom Subdomain

You will be asked to choose a unique **subdomain** for your school.

- **Example**: If you enter `greenwood`, your staff and students will access your portal at `greenwood.gurukul.com`.
- **Subdomain Rules**: Must be between 3 and 63 characters, and can only contain lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens (cannot start or end with a hyphen).

### 3. Enter School Details

Provide the core details of your organization:

- **Organization Name**: The formal name of your school (e.g., _Greenwood High School_).
- **Institution Type**: Select the category that best matches your business:
  - `SCHOOL`: For primary and secondary schools.
  - `INSTITUTE`: For colleges, universities, or higher education.
  - `COACHING`: For tutoring and specialized training centers.
- **Physical Address**: Address, city, state, country, and zip code.

### 4. Create your Admin Account

Since you are registering the institution, you will automatically be set up as the **School Owner (Super Administrator)**. Provide:

- Your full name (First and Last Name).
- A valid work email address (used for logging in).
- A secure password (minimum 8 characters).
- Your contact phone number.

### 5. Finish and Redirect

Once you submit the form, Gurukul will:

1. Create your school database records.
2. Set up your administrator account and grant you your primary tenant membership.
3. Automatically redirect you to your custom dashboard at `https://[your-subdomain].gurukul.com/dashboard`.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Because you are transitioning from the global Gurukul domain (`gurukul.com`) to your school's private portal (`your-subdomain.gurukul.com`), the page will perform a full reload. This ensures your browser session is securely initialized and locked to your school's domain context.

## Post-Registration: Managing Your Schools

After logging in to your Tenant Admin Dashboard, you can manage your educational network:

- **Multiple School Memberships**: Your dashboard displays all the tenant memberships you belong to and aggregates statistics across all your registered schools and institutes.
- **Direct Administration**: You can act as the primary administrator for each school membership directly from your account.
- **Delegate via Invites**: If you prefer to delegate, you can invite new administrators. Once they accept the email invitation, they will onboard and manage that specific school/membership on your behalf.
